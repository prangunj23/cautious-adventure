Coronavirus





Covid-19, Also known as the coronavirus, is a major virus infecting the world today. This virus originated in Wuhan, China from bats. This virus was discovered in December of 2019. It is a major concern and will cause problems if not treated carefully. Currently, it has affected 2 million people and will continue to affect the world. You can stop the spread by doing these things:

  1. Wash our hands
  2. Cover your nose and mouth
  3. Avoid close contact
  4. Stay home and self isolate
